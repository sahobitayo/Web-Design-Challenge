Max Temperature

This shows that latitudes that are located in the southern hemisphere are currently experiencing warmer weather than those in the northern hemisphere. Basically stating that that the maximum temperature increases as you get to the equator then decreases the higher you go in latitude or northward. This observation makes sense as people in the northern hemisphere experience winter, those in the southern hemisphere experience summer and vice versa.
