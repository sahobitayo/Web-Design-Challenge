Wind Speed

It does not appear that there is an obvious relationship between wind speed and latitude but it can be observed that the higher you go in latitude a slight increase in wind speed takes place. The lack of any strong relationship could be due to the fact that pressure gradient can often cause wind can move from high to low pressure, regardless of latitude. Most locations regardless of latitude have a wind speed of 20 mph or less.
